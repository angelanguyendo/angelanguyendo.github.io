Featured Services

“Taking care of the entire family – that means being able to take are of 3 generations at a time, is why I love family medicine”

Wellness Exams

  • Dr. Nguyen provides preventative care for the entire family. Starting at ages 3 months and up, teens, adults and seniors
  • Well-child exams, sports physicals and immunizations included
  • Well-woman exams, pap smears, and birth control counseling are also provided

Chronic Illness Management

  • Dr. Nguyen partners with patients for individualized care regarding chronic conditions such as: high blood pressure, diabetes, high cholesterol, heart disease, chronic kidney disease, thyroid disorders and much more

Sick Visits

  • Acute illnesses such as flu, common cold, seasonal allergies bronchitis can sometimes get in the way of every day life. Call the clinic for a same day appointment

Sexual Health and Wellness

  • Dr. Nguyen provides STD testing, PrEP, PEP, and gender care services in a safe and supportive environment

Integrative Medicine

  • As an osteopathic physician, Dr. Nguyen has received training in osteopathic manipulative treatment
  • Medical Acupuncture may be offered if appropriate
