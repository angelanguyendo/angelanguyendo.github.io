“I’m not only interested in each patient’s physical wellness but also mental and spiritual wellness. Taking care of a patient means not just listening to symptoms but also taking into account all aspects of a patient’s lifestyle.” - Dr. Angela Nguyen

As part of the Huguley Medical Associates family, Dr. Nguyen offers a variety of services for the entire family. As an osteopathic family physician, Dr. Nguyen partners with patients and takes a holistic approach to patient care while also incorporating the latest evidenced-based medical practices and latest technology.

Patients as young as 3 months can be seen. Specialized pediatric services including well child exams, school physicals, and immunizations are available. Dr. Nguyen also provides women’s wellness services including STD screening, pap smears, and contraception counseling.

Dr. Nguyen has received additional training in osteopathic manipulative treatment as well as medical acupuncture.

Dr. Nguyen is a native Texan. She grew up in Houston and received her medical degree from The University of North Texas Health Science Center Texas College of Osteopathic Medicine in Fort Worth, Texas. She then completed her residency training at The University of Texas Dell Medical School Family Medicine Residency in Austin, Texas. In her free time, she enjoys spending time with family and friends, photography, trying new places to eat in the DFW metroplex, and working out in group fitness classes.

Certified by American Board of Family Medicine

Affiliated with UNTHSC-Texas College of Osteopathic Medicine and UNTHSC-TCU School of Medicine

Member of Texas Medical Association, American Academy of Family Physicians, Tarrant County Medical Society
